About The Immortal Lion

"The Immortal Lion" is an adventurous 2D game crafted with Unity, where players take on the role of a powerful lion on a quest for survival and dominance in the wilderness. Set in a vibrant and dynamic environment, the game presents a thrilling experience of hunting, survival, and strategy.

As the lion, players navigate through lush landscapes, tracking and hunting down agile deer. The game intelligently generates diverse screens presenting challenges in every pursuit, from dense forests to open plains, offering a captivating and ever-changing gameplay experience.
